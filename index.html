A THREE-STEP EVALUATION

You are an entrepreneur in the field of energy efficiency. Changes are your business is based on a smart technology or an innovative combination of knowledge. You might be very happy with the way you run your business and the way it’s taking off in the market. But there is also a possibility your business isn’t taking off as you were expecting, and despite all the efforts, you can’t seem to get a grip on what it is that needs to be done.
Chances are the key to growth can be found in your business model. Recent research shows that many Value propositions in the field of energy effiency aren’t supported by the right business model. Companies who do have a fit business model report a better performance in the market.
